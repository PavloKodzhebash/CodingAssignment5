Kimchi is a traditional Korean fermented cabbage recipe, full of flavor and probiotics!

Ingredients

  • Napa cabbage
  • Non-iodized salt
  • Garlic cloves
  • Ginger
  • Fish sauce
  • Gochugaru (Korean chili powder)
  • Sriracha or gochujang
  • Sugar
  • Rice vinegar
  • Spring onions
  • Carrot and radishes

Equipment

  • Bowl
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Directions

  1. Cut and salt the cabbage, soak for a few hours.
  2. Make the chili paste with garlic, ginger, sauces and spices.
  3. Mix all vegetables with the chili paste.
  4. Pack into a clean jar and ferment at room temperature.
